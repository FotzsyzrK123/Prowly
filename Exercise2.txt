TC1004 - Searching word "Prowly" via Google.com (voice searching, filter: date)
Environment:
	Browser: Google Chrome v. 92.0.4515.107 (64 bit)
	Operating System: Windows
Preconditions:
- user has internet connection
- user has Browser tab opened on the Google page
- user has properly installed and working sound drivers

Step 1:
Action: User click on the microphone on the right from the search box.
Result: User can see a pop-up on the top left corner of the site asking for access to the microphone and press "Allow".

Step 2:
Action: User says "Prowly" to the microphone.
Result:	User sees the results in Google. User can see Brief information about the company in the right corner.

Step 3:
Action: User clicks on	the "tools" button and changing "any time" to "past week" to see results no older than 1 week.
Result: User is redirected to the Google results subpage, containing only past week news about Prowly.
(NOTE: Results are not ordered descending or ascending. They are mixed!)

Step 4: 
Action:	User clicks on the second result.
Result: User is redirected to the Linkleaders page generated by Prowdy
